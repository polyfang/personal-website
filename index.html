POLYFANG

A Denver based, American Jazz fusion band.
Through waves of dreamy guitar, Airy vocals, bouncy basslines and groovy synths the band seeks to explore the psychedelic frontiers of the mind.
Our sound is a kaleidoscope of colors, emotions, and textures, blended through many genres.
